第一篇 Fortune at the Fair：An Unexpected Encounter

​ In a small town, there was an annual fair that attracted a large crowd.People came from far and wide to participate in various activities and enjoy themselves. During the fair, a group of eighteen enthusiastic friends decided to try their luck at the games to earn some prizes.

Fortune - luck

Fortunate - lucky

Fortune–wealth–means

Encounter - meet

town- village - rural area 相对的 ：urban –都市 乡村和都市的交界处：outskirt 郊区

Fair 集市 Fairness 平等

​ The friends entered a competition where the winner would receive a generous dividend.The game involved carrying a plaster statue across a grey plateau while avoiding various obstacles. The carrier had to be slim and agile to navigate the course successfully.

dividend-分红，奖励 – price – reward

entered- 进入，参加

plaster statue - 水泥雕像 相近词：plastic 塑料

across a grey plateau – 穿过灰白的高原

plateau -高原

obstacles - 障碍 -difficulties

slim - 廋 - thin

agile - 灵敏的 -flexible

navigate the crouse successfully - 顺利通过这个课程

navigate - 航行，导航 相似：negative 负面的

course - 课程， 这里可以理解为航线

​ As they prepared for the challenge, the group overheard a grocer scolding a stubborn thief who had tried to steal some edible treats from his stall. The thief’s negative attitude cast a shadow over the otherwise joyful event. The friends decided to put that incident out of their minds and focus on their objective.

overheard - 偶然听到，不小心听到

grocer - 杂货店 – little store sells various simple things

scolding - 骂人

stubborn-顽固的

thief- 小偷，小贼 相似：chief 首领

steal - 偷

edible - 可以吃的，可食用的 – can be eaten

treats - 款待，零食

stall - 摊位，牲畜棚；

cast a shadow over - 蒙上了一层阴影

cast - 投射

shadow- 阴影 cast a shadow on - 给什么蒙上一层阴影

incident -accident 事件

objective - 目标 –goal - target -aim

​ In the race, the participants had to pass through a cloud of gas, which they later discovered was a harmless oxide used for dramatic effect. At an intersection,they noticed a vegetarian cafe, which was a welcome sight for the weary competitors.

race – 比赛。种族 Bai people -白人

gas -毒气，煤气

dramatic effect -特效

intersection – 岔路口

vegetarian-素食主义

cafe - 咖啡店

oxide -氧化物 oxygen -氧气

weary- 疲惫的 - tired - exhausted 来源：wear

​ As they continued, the friends encountered a landlord who had a peculiar way of pronouncing certain words. This sparked their curiosity, and they engaged him in conversation, comparing the local dialect with their own. The landlord emphasized the importance of preserving the town’s unique linguistic heritage.

landlord-地主

peculiar- 特别的 -special- strange

pronouncing - 发出声音

certain - 特定的，某些

spark curiosity - 激发好奇

engaged sb in - 使谁参与到，使谁投入到

dialect- 方言

preserve -保存 - protect -save

linguistic heritage - 语言的遗产

​ During their chat, they learned about a secret shortcut that would help them in the competition. With the landlord’s guidance, they were able to make their way through the course with incredible velocity. As they neared the finish line, they spotted a car that had been transformed into a magnificent piece of art using trash and discarded materials.

velocity - 速度

spot - 看到

magnificent piece - 伟大的艺术品

trash - 垃圾

​ Their willingness to help one another and work as a team allowed them to achieve their goal. As they crossed the finish line, they couldn’t help but feel a sense of accomplishment and pride in themselves. They had shown that,with perseverance and determination, anything was possible.

help one another - 帮助彼此

coulden’t help but - 情不自禁做某事

accomplishment - 成就 - achievement

​ In an instant,they decided to subscribe to the local league, which organized events and activities to promote the town’s culture and traditions. They realized the scope of the impact they could have on their community by being active participants and contributing their skills and talents.

scope-范围

​ As they celebrated their victory, the friends reflected on the day’s events and the valuable lessons they had learned. They vowed to continue supporting each other and working together to create a’ better future for their town. The once-innocent fair had turned into a life-changing experience that would stay with them for years to come.

reflected on -反思

vowed to -发誓 marriage vow 婚礼誓词

turn into 变成了，变为
