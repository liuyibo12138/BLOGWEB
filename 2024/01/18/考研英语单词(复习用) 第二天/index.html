第二篇 A Talented Youngster’s Journey

​ Once upon a time, there was a talented youngster named Will, who lived near the ocean. His genuine love for singing was his most remarkable trait. Everyday, he would go to the seashore and sing beautiful songs, which delighted everyone around him. His dream was to become a famous singer and perform on the grandest stages.

Youngster–youth-teenager-adolescent 青年人

genuine - 真正的 genuine Chinese food 地道中国菜 -real - authentic -sincere

remarkable - great

remark – 评论

trait–特质，特征 – feature

​ One day, Will learned about an audition in the city. He decided that it was his duty to ‘give it a try. After arriving at the designated location, he discovered that the audition was for a prestigious singing congress. The competition was fierce,and the pressure to perform well was immense.

audition - 试镜

designated – 指定的

prestigious–有声望的 respectful and famous

congress-国会

fierce – very strong - intense

immense-huge –very big

​ As he waited for his turn, Will tried to remain calm. He knew that his earnest efforts and daily practice would help him stand out from the rest. However, a group of youngsters decided to boycott the audition, claiming that the event organizers manipulated the results. This information caused some unrest among the participants.

turn 转换，轮次 is your turn 轮到你了

earnest - 认真的 - serious

stand out 站出来，脱颖而出

rest - 其他人，休息

boycott - 抵制 boycott Japanese goods 抵制日货

manipulate - control

unrest -动荡

​ Trying not to let the rumors interfere with his performance,Will focused on his talent and passion. When it was finally his turn, he stepped onto the stage, and the moment he started to sing, a harmony filled the air. The audience was mesmerized by his voice,and even the grumpiest person couldn’t help but smile.

rumor - 谣言

interfere - 干涉 interrupt or hinder

passion-enthusiasm

the audience was mesmerized – attracted

grumpiest - grumpy– angry

​ During the performance,Will felt a sudden numbness in his hand. He glanced down and saw a cable that had come loose from the sound equipment. in an instant, he used his free hand to snap the cable back into place, never missing a beat. This quick thinking saved his performance and impressed everyone in the room.

numb 麻

cable – 电缆 charge cable 充电线

snap 打响指，这里指把电线快速插回去

beat – 节拍

impressed - 留下印象

​ After the audition, the judges tallied the scores. They took into account every aspect of the participants’ performances, from their vocal range to their stage presence. The margin between the top contenders was incredibly narrow. lt was a nerve-wracking wait for Will and the other contestants.

tally - record 记录

take into account– 考虑 - consider – take into consideration

margin - difference –差额

contender - 竞争者 – contestant – competitor

narrow – 狭窄

nerve-wracking –伤脑筋 nerve– 神经 wrack –破坏

​ As luck would have it, Will’s beautiful singing and quick thinking during the cable incident earned him a spot in the congress. The news filled him with immense joy, and he vowed to work even harder from that day henceforth. His friends and family were delighted for him, and they couldn’t wait to see what the future held.

earned a spot –赢得一席之地 spot –小地方

henceforth–从此以后

what the future hold –将来会怎么怎么样

​ The journey wasn’t without its challenges, though. Will had to face criticism,and at times, he felt miserable and doubted himself. But with every attack, he learned to retort with grace and continued to work on his craft. He always remembered the moment when he stood on the stage, ready to share his gift with the world, and that memory revived his spirit.

criticism - 批评

miserable- 悲哀 - painful and feel bad

retort – 反驳 talk back

grace – elegance graceful-elegant

craft – skill

revived – 是谁复苏，振兴

​ Years later, Will became a celebrated singer, performing on grand stages and even touring on a private jet. His unwavering determination and incredible talent were an inspiration to everyone who knew him. He taught people that with hard work, perseverance, and a little bit of luck, dreams could come true.

unwavering – 毫不犹豫 waver–动摇

incredible – 不可思议的

inspiration – 鼓舞，激励

课后单词

genuine， authentic 真正的；sincere 真诚的

interrupt 打扰，阻断；hinder 阻碍

conference 会议

insulate 隔离，绝缘

wire n.线；v.接通

arrangement 安排

warfare 战争，斗争

chef 厨师；chief 首领，主要的

reveal v.揭示，显现，启示

dough 面团

pastry 糕点

consume 消费，吃

calorie 卡路里

latitude 纬度

fuel 燃料

workout 锻炼
