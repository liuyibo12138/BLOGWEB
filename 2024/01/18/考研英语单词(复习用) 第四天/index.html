第四篇 Empowering a Village: A Headmaster’s Forum on Conservation, Community, and Critical Thinking

​ Once upon a time in a small village, there was a headmaster who was known for his shrewdness and rigorous teaching methods. He was responsible for the village’s education centre , a classic dome-shaped building with a permanent exhibition of reptiles.

forum -a place for people to discuss or exchange opinion

headmaster –principal -领导 -主要的 -负责人

was known for -be famous for

shrewd -very smart - clever，wise

rigorous –strict

dome – have a round top 穹顶

permanent -永久性

reptile -爬行动物

​ One day, the headmaster decided to hold a forum on the topic of environment a conservation. The discussion included the importance of preserving water resources, especially during times of drought. The villagers were encouraged to practice water-saving measures such as using a cart to transport gallons of water, instead of wasting it on expensive and unnecessary activities.

conservation -protection conserve -protect -preserve

especially -particularly

drought – 形容词：dry

cart -small car

gallon -加仑（单位）

​ During the forum, a royal guest speaker shared her experience in leading an electrical defence project. The project aimed to reinforce security systems by using advanced technology like virtual poles and smart loudspeakers. She also discussed how the project helped to conceal sensitive information from potential acts of treason.

defence -防御

reinforce -加强

poles -电极

loudspeaker-扩音器

sensitive -敏感

conceal -hide

treason -betray your country

potential -possible -likely -liable

​ The villagers listened attentively, and soon the conversation shifted to the impact of modern society on traditional values. The headmaster highlighted the increasing discrepancy between the old and the new, using the example of analogue devices being replaced by digital ones, as well as the growing popularity of solo activities instead of community engagement.

discrepancy -gap -difference

analogy -类比，相似的

as well as -以及

​ As the discussion progressed, one of the participants brought up the issue of fake news, which seemed to be a chronic problem in their society. The headmaster, being exceedingly wise, suggested that they establish a set of guidelines to help the villagers discern the truth from lies, He also emphasized the importance of critical thinking in today’s fast-paced world, where misinformation can easily trigger negative consequences.

progress -continue

issue -problem

fake -假的

chronic -long

exceedingly wise -very smart

discern -know -distinguish

​ The villagers agreed with the headmaster’s proposal and decided toconduct a survey to gather opinions on the most pressing issues in theircommunity. They hoped that this initiative would not only raise awareness but also bring relief to those affected by the problems discussed.

proposal -a formal suggestion

conduct a survey -make a survey

initiative -措施 -action -practice -measure

relief -宽慰

​ During the forum’s closing ceremony, the headmaster congratulated everyone for their active participation and immense enthusiasm. He reminded them that the key to a successful society lies in open communication and collaboration among its members

remind -make sb think of 名词：reminder 提醒

lies in –在于

collaboration -cooperation

​ As the sun set and the villagers began to disperse, they felt a renewed sense of unity and responsibility towards their environment and community They realized that the taste of progress should not come at the cost of their precious resources and traditional values.

sun set -日落 sun up -日出

disperse -分散

taste -感觉，口味

at the cost of -以什么什么为代价

​ In the days that followed, the village witnessed a remarkable transformation. The people adopted water-saving practices, showed more interest in community activities, and engaged in open discussions on various topics. They even formed a task force to combat fake news and misinformation.

witness -see

transformation -reform -innovate -revolution

a task force -一个工作组 task -任务 force -力量，人

​ The headmaster’s forum had sparked a positive change, demonstrating thepower of education and awareness in shaping the future. The village became ashining example of a society that thrives on collective efforts, understandingand the pursuit of knowledge.

spark -trigger -stimulate

shining -闪亮的

pursuit -追寻
